Delete Your Account

Last updated: March 2026

How to delete your account

You can delete your account directly from the ReplyGen app in a few taps:

  • 1
    Open the ReplyGen app on your Android device
  • 2
    Tap the Settings tab at the bottom of the screen
  • 3
    Scroll down and tap Delete Account
  • 4
    Confirm the deletion — your account and all associated data will be removed immediately

What data is deleted

When you delete your account the following data is permanently removed:

Data Action
Email address & account credentials Deleted immediately
Monthly usage count Deleted immediately
Tone & settings preferences Deleted immediately
Screenshots & scanned text Never stored

Screenshots and conversation text are processed in real time and are never stored on our servers at any point.

Can't access the app? Email us at hello@replygen.co and we'll delete your account within 30 days.
