How it works

Three taps.
Perfect reply.

ReplyGen reads your conversation and generates replies that actually match the tone — without ever leaving the app you're in.

Step 01

Enable the bubble

Turn on the floating bubble from the ReplyGen app. It stays quietly in the corner — out of the way until you need it.

When Android asks how to share, choose Share whole screen for best results.

Step 02

AI reads the room

Tap the bubble and AI scans your screen — labelling who said what and understanding the full context of the conversation.

Step 03

Pick and paste

Three reply options appear instantly. Tap Copy and paste it straight into your chat. Need more context? Tap Scan more for older messages.
