## Full description (max 4000 chars)
AI Reply Assistant puts a smart floating bubble on your screen — tap it anytime while chatting to instantly get three AI-generated replies tailored to your conversation.

**How it works**
1. Enable the floating bubble from the app's home screen
2. Open any messaging app — WhatsApp, SMS, Telegram, Instagram DMs, and more
3. Tap the bubble to scan your conversation
4. Pick from three smart, context-aware reply suggestions
5. Tap to copy and paste into your chat — done in seconds

**AI replies that match your style**
Choose from four tone settings — Casual, Formal, Friendly, or Witty — so replies always sound like you.

**Private by design**
Screen content is processed in real time and immediately discarded. Nothing is stored. Your conversations stay yours.

**Key features**
• Floating bubble overlay that works across all apps
• One-tap screen scan with instant OCR
• Three AI reply suggestions per scan
• Tone selector: Casual · Formal · Friendly · Witty
• 50 free scans per month
• Works with WhatsApp, Messenger, SMS, Telegram, Instagram, and more

**Permissions used**
• Display over other apps — required to show the floating bubble
• Screen capture — used only when you tap the bubble to scan; never runs in background
